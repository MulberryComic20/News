Focus on content

When you want to read news you want to read news and nothing else! ReadIt helps you to focus on the content. Through a extraction method you'll only see what matters.

Easy to use

No app should be hard to use. ReadIt offers you the most simple way to get your latest news. Adding new feeds or exploring them is really easy thanks to a clean user interface.

Fast loading

A good app should be able to load fast on slow networks too. ReadIt delivers news with the fastest speed. Sites in ReadIt load many times faster than a normal website thanks to a well loading algorithm.
